Get My Pup

Get My Pup's aim is to connect animal rescue shelters with pet owners who have lost a pet. For shelters, this app serves as a tool to easily manage and maintain animal inventory. On the flip side, instead of spending countless hours on outdated shelter websites, on hold on the phone, or walking through kennels one by one at a shelter, pet owners now can simply log in to search on our up-to-date inventory in attempt to find their lost pet.

Weather App

This simple weather app simply fetches basic weather data via an api request. A user simply enters a city and state to get the temperature in Fahrenheit degrees and the wind speed in miles per hour.
